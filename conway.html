Each cell in the grid has two possible states: dead and alive. In each new generation, the configuration of cells determines which ones live or die.

Rules

  • A living cell with fewer than two or greater than three live neighbors will die.
  • A living cell with either two or three neighbors will live to see the next generation.
  • A Dead cell with exactly three live neighbors will be reborn.

Click or drag squares to toggle their states between dead and alive. After pressing start, the automation begins, and each successive generation is computed and displayed.
