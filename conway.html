Conway's Game of Life

Each cell in the grid has two possible states, dead and alive. In each new generation, the configuration of cells determines which ones live or die. See this link for more information.

Rules

  • A living cell with fewer than 2 or greater than 3 live neighbors will die.
  • A living cell with either two or three neighbors will survive into the next generation.
  • A Dead cell with exactly 3 live neighbors will become alive.

Click a square to toggle it's state between dead and alive. After pressing start, the automation begins, and each successive generation is computed and displayed.
